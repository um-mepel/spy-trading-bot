   └─ Neutral regimes: 29% of days with no clear edge

   └─ Improves win rate by avoiding mean-reversion traps

3. CAPTURE BULLISH GAINS (1.0x bullish)
   └─ Bullish regimes: 53% of days with tailwind
   └─ 2025 was a bull year - full exposure justified
   └─ Strong uptrends require full position to capture gains
